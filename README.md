# 🛒 Blinkit Sales Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an interactive **Blinkit Sales Dashboard** developed using **Microsoft Power BI**. The dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of Blinkit's sales performance, customer ratings, product categories, outlet performance, and business trends. It enables users to explore key business metrics through interactive filters and visually rich reports, helping stakeholders make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze overall sales performance.
* Compare sales across different outlet locations and outlet sizes.
* Evaluate product performance by category and fat content.
* Monitor average sales and customer ratings.
* Identify business trends over outlet establishment years.
* Build an interactive dashboard for business insights.

## 🔍 Business Insights

* Medium-sized outlets generated the highest sales.
* Tier 3 outlets contributed the largest share of total revenue.
* Supermarket Type 1 achieved the highest sales among all outlet types.
* Fruits & Vegetables and Snack Foods were among the top-performing product categories.
* Average customer rating remained close to **3.9**, indicating consistent customer satisfaction.
